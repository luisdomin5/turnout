Embed Codes

Insert the following snippets of code into your website to embed VoteAmerica's tools.

Register Tool

Direct Links

If you don't want to embed the tools in your own website, you can use the following links to associate signups with your account.

If you choose to directly link to VoteAmerica and wish to use the information collected through the VoteAmerica tools we recommend you set a Privacy Policy URL in your settings. Subscribers who list a privacy policy in their settings will have their privacy policy listed below all forms that collect data on their behalf on both embeds and the VoteAmerica website.
